What is GP 120 GSM?

GP 120 GSM refers to a Galvanised Plain sheet with a zinc coating weight of 120 grams per square metre (on both sides combined). This means each side carries approximately 60 g/m² of zinc, providing a solid barrier against corrosion.

The Z120 grade sits in the sweet spot between cost and corrosion protection, making it the default choice for most Indian applications:

  • Sufficient zinc for 15–20 years of outdoor life in normal environments
  • Costs ₹1,500–2,500/MT less than Z180, and ₹5,000–7,000/MT less than Z275
  • Available in the widest range of thicknesses and widths
  • Stocked by virtually every major mill and dealer

Frequently Asked Questions

What does 120 GSM mean in GP sheets?

GSM stands for grams per square metre. GP 120 GSM means the total zinc coating on both sides of the sheet weighs 120 grams per square metre. This is approximately 8.5 microns of zinc on each side.

Is GP 120 GSM good for roofing?

Yes! Z120 is the most commonly used grade for roofing sheets in India. In normal (non-coastal, non-industrial) environments, it provides 15–20 years of corrosion-free service life.
