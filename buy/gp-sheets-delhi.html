What is the minimum order quantity for GP sheets?

We accept orders from 1 MT onwards for ready-stock items. For specific sizes or cut-to-length, minimum 3 MT applies.

Do you deliver GP sheets to South Delhi and Noida?

Which GP sheet coating is best for roofing?

For standard roofing in Delhi NCR climate, Z120 (120 GSM) is the most popular and cost-effective. For industrial or coastal environments, Z180 or Z275 is recommended.
