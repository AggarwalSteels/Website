CR / CRCA Sheets Available in Delhi

Cold Rolled (CR) steel — also known as CRCA (Cold Rolled Close Annealed) — is produced by further rolling Hot Rolled steel at room temperature. The result is a product with superior surface finish, tighter tolerances, and better formability compared to HR steel.

Aggarwal Steels supplies CR sheets and coils to Delhi's thriving manufacturing ecosystem — from auto component makers in Okhla and Mayapuri to appliance manufacturers in Noida and electrical panel fabricators across NCR.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between CR and CRCA?

They are the same product. CRCA stands for Cold Rolled Close Annealed, which describes the full manufacturing process. CR is simply the common abbreviation.

Which CR grade is best for automotive parts?

CR3 (DDQ) for moderate forming operations, CR4 (EDDQ) for complex body panels, and CR5 (IF grade) for the most demanding deep-drawing applications.

Do you supply CR coils with Bright Annealed (BA) finish?

Yes. BA finish CR coils are available from JSW and AM/NS India in CR1 and CR2 grades. BA finish provides a mirror-like surface ideal for visible applications.
