Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order for HR plates?

1 plate (typically 2–4 MT depending on size). For cut-to-size, minimum 3 MT per specification.

Do you supply normalised plates?

Yes. Normalised plates are available for structural (E350 and above) and pressure vessel grades. Normalising improves toughness and is required for certain critical applications.

Can I get plates with UT testing?

Yes. We can arrange ultrasonic testing per IS 4260 or equivalent. UT testing adds approximately ₹1,000–2,000/MT to the cost.
