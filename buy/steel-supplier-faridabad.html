About Aggarwal Steels

Founded in 1990, Aggarwal Steels has grown from a small trading firm to one of the most respected steel suppliers in Faridabad and the Delhi National Capital Region. Over 35 years, we have built deep relationships with India's leading steel producers and a loyal client base spanning automotive, construction, HVAC, electrical, and general engineering sectors.

Our mission is simple: deliver quality steel, at competitive prices, on time, every time.
