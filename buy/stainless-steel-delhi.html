Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between SS 304 and SS 316?

SS 316 contains 2–3% molybdenum which gives it significantly better resistance to chlorides and acids. Choose 316 for marine environments, chemical exposure, or saltwater contact. For general food/beverage and indoor applications, 304 is sufficient and more economical.
